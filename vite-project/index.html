TEDxUofW 2026: Imprints at the University of Washington

TEDxUofW 2026: Imprints is the official TEDx event website for the University of Washington in Seattle, Washington. The event takes place on April 19, 2026 at Kane Hall and features speakers, performances, and student-led storytelling around ideas worth spreading.

About TEDxUofW

TEDxUofW is a student-run TEDx organization at the University of Washington that brings inspirational talks, lived experiences, and community conversations to campus in Seattle.

Featured 2026 speakers

TEDxUofW 2026 includes speakers such as Cara Hale Alter, Melissa Hutchins, Michael B. Ling, Vita Kari, Ann Frost, and Nandita Gupta.

Event details

Date: April 19, 2026. Venue: Kane Hall, University of Washington, Seattle, WA. Theme: Imprints.
