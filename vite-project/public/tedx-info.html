TEDxUofW 2026: Imprints

TEDxUofW 2026: Imprints is the official University of Washington TEDx event website for the 2026 conference in Seattle, Washington.

About the event

TEDxUofW is a student-run organization that brings TED-style talks and performances to the University of Washington community. The 2026 event theme is Imprints.

Event details

Location: Kane Hall, University of Washington, Seattle, WA
